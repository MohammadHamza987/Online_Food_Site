Frequently Asked Questions

🚚 What is the average delivery time?
Our average delivery time is 25-35 minutes, depending on your location and order complexity. We strive to deliver fresh, hot food as quickly as possible.
💳 What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, digital wallets (PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay), and cash on delivery for your convenience.
📞 How can I contact customer support?
Our friendly customer support team is available 24/7 via phone, email, and live chat. Visit our Contact page for detailed information.
🍕 Do you offer vegetarian and vegan options?
No Currently we do not offer vegetarian and vegan options, but we are working on expanding our menu to include a variety of dietary preferences. Stay tuned for updates!
🔄 What is your refund policy?
If you're not satisfied with your order, we offer full refunds within 24 hours of delivery. Your satisfaction is our top priority.
